ABOUT US

Joseline International Manpower Corporation

Joseline International Manpower Corporation (Joseline) is a licensed Philippine Recruitment Agency in the Philippines accredited by the Department of Migrant Workers (DMW).

Since commencing operations in 1976, Joseline has deployed thousands of Filipino workers equipped with world class competencies and prepared to meet the evolving demands of the global workforce. The company recruits and deploys semi-skilled, skilled, and professional workers across a diverse range of services and trades. Its established track record, supported by strong recruitment capacity and dependable deployment capability, positions the organization among the Philippines’ leading land based manpower providers.

The company places strong importance on building harmonious and long term relationships with its clients. It cultivates partnerships grounded in trust, transparency, and mutual confidence. Through this approach, Joseline International Manpower Corporation has earned recognition as a preferred recruitment partner of numerous employers, particularly across the Middle East and Asia.

As a service oriented agency, Joseline continually strengthens its systems, broadens its talent pool, and optimizes its recruitment and deployment processes to effectively meet the evolving requirements of its global partners.

Joseline stands ready to partner with your organization in meeting your workforce requirements. With its recruitment expertise, established talent network, and deployment infrastructure, the company is fully equipped to support ongoing operations as well as project based manpower requirements. It looks forward to fostering a productive partnership anchored on reliability, efficiency, and mutual growth.
